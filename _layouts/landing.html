Background

How I got here

Ten years at Amazon, then I quit to do all of it myself.

The Amazon Years

Rewrote a labor planning engine from a research paper. Shipped a scheduling platform for 60K people in 8 months. Built a weather response system that killed a beloved spreadsheet process. Connected PLCs to the cloud. Mostly I just kept fixing the thing nobody else wanted to touch.

Since Leaving

{{ site.data.ventures | size }} ventures since January. I design PCBs in KiCad, write embedded firmware, deploy cloud infra, and sometimes all three in the same week. I like switching contexts. Sue me.

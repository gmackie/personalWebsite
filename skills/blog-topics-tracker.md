## Writing Strategy

### Blog as Knowledge Repository
- **Document as you go**: Write posts while projects are fresh
- **Focus on lessons learned**: What would you want to remember in 6 months?
- **Include failures and pivots**: These are often the most valuable insights
- **Link to related projects**: Cross-reference your work for easy navigation

### Content for Social Media Amplification
- **Twitter threads**: Pull 3-5 key insights from each post
- **YouTube videos**: Use posts as detailed scripts/references
- **LinkedIn**: Professional development angle on learning posts
- **GitHub**: Link to relevant repos and technical details

### Realistic Publishing Goals
- **1 post per month**: Sustainable pace that builds momentum
- **Mix post types**: Rotate between technical, personal, and learning topics
- **Quality over quantity**: Better to have fewer, well-documented posts
